About Us

Created by fathers – not lawyers – who've lived it

Our Story

Who We Are

Dad's Corner was created by fathers – not lawyers – who have lived through the stress and emotional weight of the family court system. We know firsthand how isolating and overwhelming it can feel to navigate high-conflict parenting situations, time-sharing disputes, relocation issues, and the challenge of enforcing court orders.

Our goal is simple: to help other dads feel more informed, supported, and empowered – and not alone.

What We Offer

The content on Dad's Corner is based on our real-life experiences with family law proceedings. While we are not attorneys and nothing here constitutes legal advice, we offer:

  • Educational resources on time-sharing, parenting plans, and co-parenting
  • Tips and stories from dads who have been through similar situations
  • Guidance on how to stay present and connected with your children
  • Community events and support for fathers at every stage of the journey

Our Philosophy

We believe that family is important, and everyone's individual voice should be, too. Every dad's story is unique, and we're here to listen as much as we are to share.

Strong connections and support create a nurturing environment for everyone – and that starts with dads who show up. Whether you're navigating a difficult custody situation or simply looking for ways to create more cherished family moments, you're in the right place.

Our Focus

Dad's Corner is especially focused on helping fathers in Florida who are dealing with family court matters. Our educational content is based on experiences in Florida family law proceedings, including time-sharing under Florida Statute 61.13 and co-parenting in high-conflict situations.

That said, the values, stories, and family tips we share are for dads everywhere.
