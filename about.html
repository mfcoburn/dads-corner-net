About Me

The person behind Dad's Corner

Hi there!

Who Am I?

Welcome to Dad's Corner – a small slice of the internet that's been mine for a while now. I'm a husband, a father, a weekend tinkerer, and someone who genuinely enjoys sharing what I've learned (and what's gone wrong).

This site started as a place to keep notes for myself and grew into something I'm proud to share with others. If any of it is useful to you – great. If it just makes you smile – even better.

What I Do

By day I work in technology. By evenings and weekends I'm usually:

  • Working on something in the garage or the backyard
  • Trying to teach my kids something useful (or learning something from them)
  • Cooking something that may or may not have followed a recipe
  • Fishing, hiking, or otherwise being outside whenever possible

Why Dad's Corner?

Because every dad needs a corner. A place to put your tools, your thoughts, and your half-finished projects. This is mine – except it lives on the internet.

I've been running some version of this site for years. It's changed a lot over time, and it keeps evolving. I hope you enjoy what you find here.
