Weekly Wednesday Meeting

Dad's Corner is a peer‑led, weekly Zoom gathering for fathers navigating the family court system. The group meets Wednesday evenings from 6:30–7:30 PM and is built around open, supportive conversations between dads who understand the challenges of co‑parenting, custody, and showing up as a father during difficult circumstances.

This is not a lecture or a program—it's a space for fathers to connect, share perspectives, and support one another through real conversation and mutual respect. Participation is free, virtual, and focused on community, encouragement, and shared experience.

Built by dads, for dads – and every family.

Dad's Corner was created by fathers who understand firsthand how important it is to stay connected, informed, and supported. We believe that family is important, and everyone's individual voice should be, too.

Whether you're looking for family activity ideas, co-parenting guidance, or just a community that understands what you're going through – you've found the right place.
